The Order of the Day

Schedule of Events

  • 3:00 pm

    Guests Arrive

    Welcome drinks and vineyard views. Park at Stone Tower and follow the path to the ceremony grounds.

  • 4:00 pm

    Ceremony

    An outdoor ceremony among the vines. Please be seated by 3:45 PM. In case of rain, the ceremony moves to the covered terrace.

  • 4:30 pm

    Cocktail Hour

    Hors d'oeuvres, wine tastings, and live acoustic music on the terrace. Mix, mingle, and explore the vineyard.

  • 6:00 pm

    Dinner & Reception

    A seated dinner under the stars, followed by toasts, first dances, and a celebration that goes ever on.

  • 9:00 pm

    Dancing & Dessert

    Dancing into the night alongside a dessert bar and late-night bites. The adventure is far from over.

  • 11:00 pm

    Farewell

    The road goes ever on — but for tonight, we part until next time.

The Lands of the Shire

Stone Tower Winery

Nestled in the rolling hills of Loudoun County, Stone Tower Winery sits on 300 acres of Virginia wine country. Its stone-clad barn, sweeping vineyard views, and lush countryside make it feel like a place straight out of Middle-earth — rustic, timeless, and beautiful.
